Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ItemValue

What is ItemValue?
ItemValue is an AI-powered tool that helps you decide whether to sell items on eBay or donate them. We analyze real marketplace data to give you accurate price estimates, calculate fees and shipping costs, and tell you your potential profit.
How accurate are the price estimates?
Our estimates are based on actual completed sales on eBay. We search for items similar to yours and analyze what they actually sold for, not just listing prices. This gives you realistic expectations for what your item could sell for.

Scans must be used within your access period (30, 60, or 90 days depending on your bundle). Unused scans do not roll over after expiration. You can add the year extension at checkout to get 365 days instead.
