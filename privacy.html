Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect:

  • Item descriptions you enter for analysis
  • Email address if you provide it or make a purchase
  • Payment information processed securely through Stripe
  • Usage data such as number of scans used

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide item analysis and recommendations
  • Process payments and deliver purchased scan bundles
  • Send transactional emails (purchase confirmations, access codes)
  • Send marketing emails if you opt in
  • Improve our Service

3. Data Storage

Your analysis history is stored locally in your browser (localStorage). We do not store your item descriptions on our servers after analysis is complete.

4. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Stripe for payment processing
  • Resend for email delivery
  • SerpAPI for marketplace data
  • Anthropic Claude for AI analysis

These services have their own privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption and secure payment processing.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Clear your local history at any time using the app
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us

7. Cookies

We use localStorage for app functionality. We do not use tracking cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Check this page for the latest version.

10. Contact

Questions about privacy? Contact us through the app or website.
